ATEC Workshop

Supervisor:

CHUA David


This cell hosts the state-of-the-art Automatic Test Equipment (ATEC) stations.
Automatic Test Programs troubleshoot a wide range of Avionics Computers from a large number of Aircraft Systems.

ATEC stations work round the clock in a temperature and humidity controlled environment.

The ATEC 5000 and Series 6 can test numerous SAFRAN and 3rd parties equipment from various Aircraft type such as ATR 42-72, Airbus A320, A330, A380 or Boeing B777 and B787.

For example, the SAFRAN Landing System first electrical Braking Computer (eBAC) for the Boeing Dreamliner B787 is repaired in this workshop.

Ventilation Systems Workshop

Supervisor:

ONG Chi Ping


This workshop is dedicated to the Test, Repair and Overhaul of the electro-mechanical equipment such as Fans, Valves for ventilation of Avionics Bays and Aircraft Cabins or Vacuum Blower.

These components are used in harsh environment on the Aircraft and can generate high level noise. Thus the testing is performed in sound proof rooms.

The repair shop is ready to accommodate equipments from the Airbus A350 SAFRAN Ventilation Systems. Also the SAFRAN Electronics & Defense rotary Trim Actuators and Linear Stick Pushers are tested and fine-tuned using a fully integrated Universal Actuator Test Bench.

APU Gen Workshop

Supervisor:

NONIS William-Francis


This cell performs Test, Repair and Overhaul of Power Generation equipments for SAFRAN Electrical & Power, such as Auxiliary Power Unit (APU), AC and DC generators, Starter generators, as well as the Avionics Ground Power Units.

Consolidated in a unique site, this workshop hosts improved up-to-date test fixtures delivering best in class repair services to Customers.









Radio / Radar Workshop

Supervisor:

KOH Wan-Hoon


This workshop performs Testing, Repair & Overhaul of all Communication and Navigation Components.

The major systems tested in this workshop comprise of the Weather Radar, Transceiver and Antenna.

The Communication repairs performed include the Distance Measuring Equipment (DME), Automatic Direction Finder (ADF), Instrument Landing System (ILS), Traffic Alert and Collision Avoidance System (TCAS) and Emergency Locator Transmitter (ELT).









Recorder / Read-Out Workshop

Supervisor:

KOH Wan-Hoon


This site performs Testing, Repair & Overhaul of critical Digital Flight Data Recorders (DFDR) and Cockpit Voice Recorders (CVR).

Also SAFRAN Electronics & Defense Services Asia works in conjunction with Airworthiness Authorities and Investigation Bureaus on adhoc basis.
In addition, we provide Airlines customers with high added-value Read-Out services for Flight Optimization.
